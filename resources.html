Application Guide

Mastering the components of your college application.

Application Checklist

Required: Transcripts, Recommendations (2-3), SAT/ACT scores, Essays.

Optional: Portfolios, Research papers, AP Scores.

Deadline: Nov 1 (ED/EA) or Jan 1-15 (RD)

Testing Strategy

SAT: Target 1450+ for top tier. 3-6 months prep.

ACT: Target 32-36. Science section advantage.

Note: Many schools remain test-optional.

Essay Writing

Common App: 650 words. Focus on personal growth & identity.

Supplementals: "Why us?", "Why major?". Be specific.

Tip: Authentic voice > Big words.

Financial Aid

FAFSA: Federal aid. Opens Oct 1.

CSS Profile: Institutional aid. More detailed.

Types: Merit scholarships, Need-based grants.

Success Timeline

A strategic roadmap for your high school journey.

10

Sophomore Year (Grade 10)

Build your foundation. Maintain a 3.5+ GPA, explore extracurriculars, and take the PSAT for practice.

11

Junior Year (Grade 11)

Critical year. Take SAT/ACT, maximize AP/IB course rigor, and seek leadership roles. Build your initial college list of 15-20 schools.

Summer Before Senior Year

Draft essays, finalize your list to 8-12 schools, and visit campuses. Start the Common App early.

12

Senior Fall (Grade 12)

Submit ED/EA applications by Nov 1. Request transcripts and recommendations. Submit CSS Profile/FAFSA.

Senior Spring (Grade 12)

Submit Regular Decision apps (Jan). Await decisions (Mar/Apr). Compare offers and commit by May 1st!

Comprehensive Application Timeline

1

Sophomore Year (Grade 10)

• Focus on maintaining strong GPA (3.5+ recommended)
• Explore potential majors and career interests
• Begin building extracurricular profile
• Take PSAT/NMSQT for practice
• Start researching colleges and universities

2

Junior Year (Grade 11)

• Take SAT/ACT (spring semester)
• Maintain rigorous course load with AP/IB classes
• Develop leadership in extracurricular activities
• Create preliminary college list (15-20 schools)
• Attend college fairs and virtual tours
• Request teacher recommendations (spring)

3

Summer Before Senior Year

• Finalize college list (8-12 schools)
• Begin working on Common App essay
• Complete resume/activity list
• Visit campuses if possible
• Prepare for possible SAT/ACT retakes
• Research scholarship opportunities

4

Senior Year - Fall Semester

• Submit Early Decision/Early Action applications (Nov 1)
• Complete remaining applications
• Request official transcripts
• Follow up on recommendations
• Prepare for interviews if required
• Submit CSS Profile if applying for aid

5

Senior Year - Spring Semester

• Submit Regular Decision applications (Jan 1-15)
• Complete FAFSA (opens Oct 1)
• Await admission decisions (March-April)
• Compare financial aid packages
• Make final college decision by May 1
• Submit enrollment deposit

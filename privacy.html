Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Studyflow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Studyflow, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the data practices described, you should not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile information
  • Educational Data: Course information, grades, assignments, exam schedules, and academic performance data
  • Usage Data: Study habits, app usage patterns, and feature interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information

2.2 Data Collection Methods

  • Information you provide directly when creating an account or using features
  • Automatically collected data through app usage and analytics
  • Data from educational institutions (with proper authorization)
  • Third-party integrations (when explicitly authorized by you)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our educational services
  • Personalize your learning experience and provide tailored recommendations
  • Analyze academic performance and provide insights
  • Communicate with you about app updates, features, and educational content
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

Educational Purpose: All data collection and processing is strictly for educational purposes to enhance learning outcomes and academic performance.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 When We Share Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Educational Institutions: When connected to your school's learning management system (with proper authorization)
  • With Parents/Guardians: For student accounts, limited information may be shared with connected parent accounts
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

4.2 Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure data storage practices

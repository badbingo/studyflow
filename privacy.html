Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how Studyflow protects and manages your personal information.

1. Introduction

Studyflow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Studyflow, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the data practices described, you should not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile information
  • Educational Data: Course information, grades, assignments, exam schedules, and academic performance data
  • Usage Data: Study habits, app usage patterns, and feature interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information

2.2 Data Collection Methods

  • Information you provide directly when creating an account or using features
  • Automatically collected data through app usage and analytics
  • Data from educational institutions (with proper authorization)
  • Third-party integrations (when explicitly authorized by you)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our educational services
  • Personalize your learning experience and provide tailored recommendations
  • Analyze academic performance and provide insights
  • Communicate with you about app updates, features, and educational content
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

Educational Purpose: All data collection and processing is strictly for educational purposes to enhance learning outcomes and academic performance.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 When We Share Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Educational Institutions: When connected to your school's learning management system (with proper authorization)
  • With Parents/Guardians: For student accounts, limited information may be shared with connected parent accounts
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

4.2 Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure data storage practices

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the services you have requested
  • Comply with our legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Support educational research and improvement (in anonymized form)

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of certain data collection and marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

7. Children's Privacy

Studyflow is designed for educational use and may be used by children under 13 with appropriate parental consent. We comply with:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)
  • Other applicable child privacy laws

For student accounts, we:

  • Require verifiable parental consent for children under 13
  • Limit data collection to educational purposes only
  • Provide parental access and control over children's accounts
  • Do not use children's data for advertising purposes

8. International Data Transfers

Studyflow operates globally, and your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

9. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive educational data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Secure data centers with physical security controls
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in our application
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our information practices.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@studyflow.app
  • Address: Studyflow Inc., 123 Education Street, San Francisco, CA 94102
  • In-app Support: Use the help section within the Studyflow application

We typically respond to privacy-related inquiries within 48 hours.

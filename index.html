For Students

Focus, Finish, Feel Proud

Studyflow Club helps students take control of their learning and build confidence through visible progress.

  • Plan & Prioritize: Organize homework, exams, and study goals with clear priorities.
  • Manageable Steps: Break big tasks into simple, manageable study sessions.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Stay focused with customizable study and break intervals.
  • Track Progress: Easy-to-read charts showing growth over time.
  • Notes & Logs: Keep notes and revision logs right next to tasks.
  • Connect & Share: Share tips and celebrate milestones with classmates.
  • Earn Rewards: Turn completed goals into reward requests approved by parents.
  • AI Quick Input: Snap a photo of your timetable or use voice to add homework instantly.

Less stress. More focus. Real motivation.

For Parents

Support Without Stress

Studyflow Club helps parents guide and encourage learning—without hovering or constant reminders.

  • Family Hub: Link and manage up to 4 students from one account.
  • Visibility: View schedules, deadlines, and progress at a glance.
  • Encourage: Send messages and timely reminders.
  • Approve Rewards: Approve rewards tied to completed goals, reinforcing positive habits.
  • Supportive: Stay involved in a structured way—without micromanaging.

See effort clearly. Celebrate progress confidently.
